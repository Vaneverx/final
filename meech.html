Meech's Story

A rescue that became impossible to forget

Home Ace Spooky

A couple months after Spooky’s first birthday, I received a message from a friend I had not spoken to since graduating high school. She explained that a woman had discovered a box of abandoned kittens left outside near her house and was desperately trying to find help for them.

My friend asked if there was any way I could foster one of the kittens or help rehome them while they searched for a permanent family. Without hesitation, I immediately agreed. I could not imagine leaving such a tiny kitten without care or support.

When I first saw her, she was incredibly small and adorable, and we temporarily named her Meech. Even though we knew she would eventually go to another home, we treated her like part of the family from the very beginning. During those two weeks, we made sure she felt safe, loved, and cared for while she adjusted to being indoors and around people.

Eventually, we were able to find Meech a loving home where she could continue growing up safely. Although she was only with us for a short amount of time, rescuing and fostering her reminded me that even temporary acts of kindness can completely change an animal’s life.
